Geo-informatics and Space Technology Development Agency (GISTDA)

I am an engineer in the Geo-informatics and Space Technology Agency (GISTDA), Bangkok, Thailand. My research interests include remote sensing, computer vision. More specifically, I am interested in applying data analysis technique and machine learning to extract meaningful information from geo-spatial data.
